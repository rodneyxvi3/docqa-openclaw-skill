Vacation and Time Off Policy

Full-time employees accrue fifteen days of paid vacation per calendar year
during their first two years of employment. After two years of continuous
service, the accrual rate increases to twenty days per year. Vacation days
accrue monthly and unused days may be carried over into the next year up to
a maximum of ten days.

To request vacation, submit a time-off request through the HR portal at
least five business days before the requested start date. Requests longer
than two consecutive weeks require manager approval at least three weeks in
advance.

Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements are evaluated on a role-by-role basis and require
sign-off from both the manager and HR. Remote employees must be reachable
during core hours, which are 10 AM to 3 PM in their local time zone.

Sick Leave

Employees receive ten paid sick days per year, which do not carry over and
do not require advance notice. A doctor's note is required for absences
longer than three consecutive days.
