Product FAQ

What plans are available?
We offer three plans: Starter, Growth, and Enterprise. Starter is free for
up to two team members. Growth costs 29 dollars per user per month and
includes unlimited projects and priority email support. Enterprise adds
single sign-on, an audit log, and a dedicated account manager, with pricing
available on request.

Does the product support offline use?
The desktop app caches your most recent workspace and lets you keep editing
without a connection. Changes sync automatically the next time you're
online. The mobile app currently requires a connection for all actions.

How do I export my data?
Go to Settings, then Data Export. You can export your workspace as a CSV,
JSON, or PDF. Exports larger than 500 megabytes are emailed as a download
link instead of downloading directly in the browser.

What is the uptime guarantee?
Growth and Enterprise plans include a 99.9 percent uptime service level
agreement, credited automatically to your account if we fall short in a
given month. Starter plan usage is best-effort with no formal SLA.
